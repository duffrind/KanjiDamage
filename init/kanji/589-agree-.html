賛 (agree) + 成 (become) = 賛成 (agree/ support)

to agree with a statement - not just agree but also support, as in "Many people support this new law." "Most of the committe members support the chairman's fuckin' proposal." ( in this case, it is usually used about a great number of people, not just one individual)
