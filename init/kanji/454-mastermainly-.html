主語(しゅご)

subject ★★★☆☆
主 (master/mainly) + 語 (language) = 主語 (subject)

the subject of the sentence. AKA the thing Japanese people never say.
XXX 主義(しゅぎ)

- ism ★★★☆☆ SUF
主 (master/mainly) + 義 (righteousness) = 主義 (- ism)

SUFFIX meaning 'ism'. As in capitalism, communism, materialism, and a Japanese grindcore band called 'intestine baalism.'

共産主義(きょうさんしゅぎ)

communism ★★☆☆☆
共 (with) + 産 (childbirth, production of things) + 主義 (- ism) = 共産主義 (communism)

無政府主義(むせいふしゅぎ)

@narchy ★★☆☆☆
無 (without) + 政府 (government) + 主義 (- ism) = 無政府主義 (@narchy)

民主主義(みんしゅしゅぎ)

democracy ★★☆☆☆
民 (folk) + 主 (master/mainly) + 主義 (- ism) = 民主主義 (democracy )

資本主義(しほんしゅぎ)

capitalism ★★☆☆☆
資本 (capital) + 主義 (- ism) = 資本主義 (capitalism)

飼い主(かいぬし)

pet owner ★☆☆☆☆
飼 (keep a pet) + 主 (master/mainly) = 飼い主 (pet owner)

封建主義(ほうけんしゅぎ)

feudalism ☆☆☆☆☆
封 (seal in) + 建 (build) + 主義 (- ism) = 封建主義 (feudalism)

theme song ☆☆☆☆☆
