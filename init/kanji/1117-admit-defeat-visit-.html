参 admit defeat / visit

ム (moocow) + 大 (big) + (hair)
★★★☆☆
8 strokes
JERK

Onyomi

SAN

Mnemonic

SANTA visits a moocow with big hair. (He shaves it for his false beard)

Kunyomi

( が or に ) まい＊る When you say まいた！！ it means, to say uncle, or　!No mas! (Confusingly, it can also mean 'I'm fed up!' 'Fuck this, I'm outta here.')

However, there's another meaning: to visit an important place.　（luckily, this JERK meaning is very formal and almost never used anymore)

What is really fucked up is, usually when a word has 2 meanings, you can tell which meaning it is by the particle. But with MAIRU, both meanings use NI. NP1/2 KANA
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

参加(さんか) する

join ★★★☆☆
参 (admit defeat / visit) + 加 (add) = 参加 (join)

to join a group, or participate in something
が 降参(こうさん) する

surrender ★★☆☆☆
降 (descend /rainfall / get out of vehicle ) + 参 (admit defeat / visit) = 降参 (surrender)

a casual word for surrender: to give up mid-game. A politician concedes defeat. To say uncle.
に 参拝(さんぱい) する

go to a temple to pray ☆☆☆☆☆ NP
参 (admit defeat / visit) + 拝 (worship) = 参拝 (go to a temple to pray)
