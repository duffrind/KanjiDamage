陰 shady

(town) + 今 (now) + (decapitated cow)
★★★☆☆
11 strokes
DUPE
Unlike the OTHER shadow kanji (影) which is literal, this shadow kanji (陰) is more figurative: things that are shady, dark, secret, or hidden.

陰 is also the kanji used for naughty bits.

Onyomi

IN

Mnemonic

Right now this town is full of decapitated cows, lurking IN the shadows.

Kunyomi

かげ shadow, both literally , and more likely, metaphorically. If someone is operating shady deals from the shadows, you use this kanji. 1/2 KANA
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

xxx　の お陰で(おかげで)

thanks to XXX ★★★☆☆ 1/2 KANA

thanks to XXX (despite 陰　 being the 'bad shadow' kanji, お陰で　is not only used with bad things. You can say, thanks to you, I have mastered the techniques of Double Dutch, and it would be fine.)

sex organs ☆☆☆☆☆ NP

clitoris ☆☆☆☆☆ SARC
陰 (shady) + 核 (nucleus) = 陰核 (clitoris)

clitoris - not a word you'd use in most conversations, but I'm including it anyway because the literal translation is so irresistable: hidden nucleus! How boss is that?
