Kunyomi

まれ ( な ) very similar to めずらしい(unusual), but more formal/newspapery. Also has the connotation of rare-because-it's-valuable (endangered species, a rare talent, etc.) If you see a dog-turd shaped like Lyle Lovett, that's 珍しい , not まれ。 1/2 KANA

Jukugo

希望(きぼう) する

request ★★★☆☆
希 (request / uncommon) + 望 (desire) = 希望 (request)

A common-place, easily possible wish: "I'd like an aisle seat." "I'd like to fly nonstop."　希望 might be better translated as 'request': 'Customers wishing to request a child-seat for their infant please see the waiter.'
希少(きしょう) な

rare animals or plants ☆☆☆☆☆ NP
希 (request / uncommon) + 少 (a little (amount)) = 希少 (rare animals or plants)

useless, academic word for rare animals or plants.
