顔 face

立 (stand up) + (cliff) + (hair) + (headright) Same-ON
★★★★★
18 strokes
This is an odd kanji because, although it has a bunch of jukugo, it uses the KUN in all of them!

Onyomi

Mnemonic

You stand on a cliff, the hair from your head blowing into your face.

Kunyomi

かお face
★★★★★

Jukugo

ｘｘｘ 顔をした(かおをした)

facial expression ★★★★☆

"She made a sour face" = sour顔をした。 "He made a mad face"= mad顔をした。
笑顔(えがお)

smiling face ★★★☆☆ FP
笑 (laugh) + 顔 (face) = 笑顔 (smiling face)

似顔絵(にがおえ)

portrait ★★☆☆☆ KUNKUN
似 (resemble) + 顔 (face) + 絵 (picture) = 似顔絵 (portrait)

横顔(よこがお)

face in profile ☆☆☆☆☆ KUNKUN
横 (side / arrogant) + 顔 (face) = 横顔 (face in profile)
