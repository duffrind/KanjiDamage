豊 plentiful

曲 (song / turn or bend) + 豆 (bean)
★★☆☆☆
13 strokes
PN

Onyomi

HOU

HOES are plentiful in this region.

Mnemonic

If you play a song I will reward you with plentiful magic beans.

Kunyomi

ゆた*か ( な ) abundant - like a rainforest abundant in life or a country's abundant natural resources. You can also use it for a person - he has a lot of education or a wealth of wisdom. (but not used for actual dollars - in that case you'd use 裕福 (yuufuku)。）
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

豊満(ほうまん) な

voluptuous ★☆☆☆☆ ABU
豊 (plentiful) + 満 (full) = 豊満 (voluptuous)

not what it sounds like! Houman means zatfig, voluptuous. This has two meanings： a) a borderline sexual-harassment word used generally about the big titty women. b) a a relatively polite way to describe a chubby woman. Anyway, ABU!
豊富(ほうふ) なxxx

rich in xxx ★☆☆☆☆
豊 (plentiful) + 富 (get rich) = 豊富 (rich in xxx)

abundant, rich in xxx (resources, cows, etc.)
