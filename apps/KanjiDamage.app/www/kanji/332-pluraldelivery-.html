達 plural/delivery

(motion) + 幸 (luck, happiness)
★★★★★
12 strokes
BETAJERKSUF
JERK: Normally, this means "the delivery arrives" but half the time means "plural." There is no easy way to tell which it means.

Onyomi

TATSU/ TACHI

I was TAUGHT TO use plurals correctly, therefore I am TOUCHY when people fuck them up.

Mnemonic

It's considered happy to move in pairs because you have a friend.

Jukugo

友達(ともだち)

friend ★★★★★
友 (friend) + 達 (plural/delivery) = 友達 (friend)

私達(わたしたち)

us ★★★★☆ 1/2 KANA
私 (me / private) + 達 (plural/delivery) = 私達 (us)

配達(はいたつ) する

deliver ★★★☆☆
配 (distribute) + 達 (plural/delivery) = 配達 (deliver)

to deliver. Used about business - used when money changes hands. Deliver by truck or UPS or FEDEX.
XXX 達(たち)

more than one of XXX. ★★★☆☆ SUF

SUFFIX, meaning "more than one of XXX."But unlike English, you can't pluralize just any word.

In fact 90% of the time you'll see 達, it's in one of these 3 situations: 1) 仲間達：なかまたち：　the group of friends. 2) 人達：ひとたち： people, and 3) 子供達：こどもたち： the children.
xxxの 達人(たつじん)

master of xxx ★★☆☆☆
達 (plural/delivery) + 人 (person) = 達人 (master of xxx)
