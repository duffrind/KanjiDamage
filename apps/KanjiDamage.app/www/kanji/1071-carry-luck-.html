I wish the army luck when they move forward with their campaign - hopefully they only meet UN-armed civilians.

Kunyomi

はこ*ぶ to transport - unlike はいたつ(配達）, 運ぶ is NOT related to business or money. If you help your friend move his fridge to his new place, that's 運ぶ. If you pick up your suitcase and take it to the airport, that's 運ぶ。
