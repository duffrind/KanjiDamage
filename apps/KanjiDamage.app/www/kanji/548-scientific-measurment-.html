測 scientific measurment

(waterleft) + 則 (rule) Same-ON
★★☆☆☆
12 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

SOKU

We measured the bacteria percolating in his SOCKS.

Mnemonic

My rule is to measure your blood and water.

Kunyomi

はか＊る to measure, but (unlike the other two はかる　kanji) THIS はかる　 usually used in a medical or scientific context. (blood pressure, air pressure).

Also used for area, distance, sound decibels, angle, capacity.
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

推測(すいそく) する

speculate. ★☆☆☆☆ NP
推 (infer) + 測 (scientific measurment) = 推測 (speculate.)

speculate. Kind of formal: "It's too early to speculate about the economy." "Scientists speculate that..."
