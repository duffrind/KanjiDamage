流 flow

(waterleft) + (decapitated cow) + 川 (river)
★★★☆☆
10 strokes

Onyomi

RYUU

Don't REUSE your flow so much, it'll sound wack. You gotta keep coming up with new styles.

Mnemonic

The river's flow carried the decapitated cow-head downstream , bobbing up and down in the water.

Kunyomi

( を ) なが＊す various meanings. . . .to make something flow - to flush a toilet. - Also means, a DJ plays music. Which is pretty rad.
★★★☆☆
( が ) なが＊れる something is literlly flowing. Also used when music is playing on the loudspeaker, or when time passes (時間が流れると。。。。）(literally, 'In the course of time . . .. ').
★★★☆☆
